Sand

And you trembled,
and we sat on the beach.
For a moment I thought
you already knew.
But then you kissed my nose, looked in my eyes
and it was so obscene
I wanted to cry
and dig a hole in the sand
and bury myself. So I did.
And you shoved me, and I landed next to
a sea urchin, and you broke your skin
so I could kiss you,
and we fell apart on the shore.
