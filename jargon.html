Jargon

As I kiss your eyes closed
I reach into your sleeves
and trace the crevices of your back
I feel you rushing my cheek
And I lace my fingers in your hair
And touch your hot scalp
and press you closer
I drink your tears
caress the back of your ear
nibble on your neck
As I hold the left side of your face
and my thumb comes
dangerously close to your eye
Almost
I've come to associate that word with you
Till now

You leave me wishing for more
