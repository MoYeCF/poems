Day of

Rough hives shave my skin
a painful slot of
melancholy and
thought
I wonder what you're like when you're happy
When I told you I know I've fallen in love
when I take an interest in anything you do,
I was lying. I was just glad to be done with it all.
In the darkness I wouldn't spot you
saturated in my thoughts
heaps of receipts
"take flight"
so many tires
I see light and I anticipate
and I can't help but wish I was someone else.
Gaze cast downward
eternally lonely, it seems
