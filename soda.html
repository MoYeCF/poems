Soda

When some of the moisture have not yet evaporated,
dip your thumb halfway into the opening of a can of seltzer;
press down, if you dare. Channel the gears of the mundane,
and squeeze down on it, until you can barely feel.
In one fell swoop, swish your second digit toward you.
Harvest and savour the glory as your skin flakes.
Sometimes there is blood, which means you can't throw for a few days,
but just control it.
Oh, and be sure to squeeze. Hard. Empty the tip of your thumb.
Do not wipe it off. You might get someone HIV. Suck on it, instead.
Run yourself dry. The burn will turn to satisfaction. You have healed again.
